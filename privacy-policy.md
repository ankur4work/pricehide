# Privacy Policy for Xeo Hide Price

**Last Updated: March 2, 2026**

## Introduction

Xeo Hide Price ("we", "our", "the app") is a Shopify application that automatically hides product prices when inventory reaches zero. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you install and use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Store Information
When you install Xeo Hide Price, we access the following information from your Shopify store:

- **Store domain** — To identify your store and save your app settings
- **Product data** (read-only) — To check product inventory status
- **Inventory levels** (read-only) — To determine when products are out of stock
- **Theme information** (read-only) — To enable the app embed in your store's theme

### App Settings
We store the configuration preferences you set within the app, including:

- Whether price hiding is enabled or disabled
- Your custom "out of stock" message text
- Display preferences (which pages to hide prices on)
- Whether to hide the "Add to Cart" button

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect any customer personal data
- We do **not** collect payment or credit card information
- We do **not** collect email addresses, names, or contact details of your customers
- We do **not** use cookies or tracking technologies on your storefront
- We do **not** sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. Provide the core functionality of hiding prices for out-of-stock products
2. Save and apply your app configuration settings
3. Monitor inventory changes via webhooks to update price visibility in real time

## Data Storage

- App settings are stored in a secure database associated with your store domain
- We do not store any customer data
- We do not store any product data beyond what is needed for real-time functionality

## Data Retention

- Your app settings are retained as long as the app is installed on your store
- When you uninstall the app, all associated settings and data are automatically deleted

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the information we store, including:

- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- OAuth 2.0 authentication for Shopify API access
- No storage of Shopify API tokens beyond active sessions

## Third-Party Services

Xeo Hide Price does not share any data with third-party services. The app communicates only with:

- **Shopify API** — To read product/inventory data and manage the app embed
- **Your Shopify storefront** — To apply price hiding on your store's front end

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — View all stored settings within the app dashboard
- **Modify** your data — Change any settings at any time through the app
- **Delete** your data — Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data

## GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect or store any personal customer data, there is no personal data to process. We handle the following mandatory Shopify webhooks:

- **Customer Data Request** — Acknowledged (no customer data stored)
- **Customer Data Erasure** — Acknowledged (no customer data stored)
- **Shop Data Erasure** — All app settings are deleted upon request

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
